EDUCATION AND TRAINING

Through our local partners, we focus on three main hygiene behaviors: handwashing at critical times; safe management of domestic water; and hygienic management and use of latrines and safe management of excreta. We motivate communities to improve their hygiene behavior, promote hygiene as important for good health and provide handwashing facilities.

HEALTH

Safe water, a clean living environment and a clean body are essential for good human health. These basic conditions are often absent in the communities where we work, which has a tremendous effect on the health of the people in these communities. Our work on health aims to increase the prioritization of water, sanitation and hygiene in national and community health goals, policies and strategies, and to educate the communities in relevant health programmes and interventions.

About us

Africa Water and Sanitation Foundation is an international organization whose mission is to transform the lives of the poorest and most marginalized people by improving access to clean water, sanitation and hygiene in Africa.
